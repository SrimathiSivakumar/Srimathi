Wooden junks: Traditional wooden junks are iconic boats used for centuries in Halong Bay and other coastal areas of Vietnam. These boats feature distinctive curved sails and are often used for tourism, offering overnight cruises and day trips for visitors to explore the stunning limestone karsts and islands. River ferries: In Vietnam's riverine regions, ferries are crucial in transporting people, vehicles, and goods across waterways. These ferries range from small, manually operated boats to larger motorized vessels capable of carrying trucks and buses. river-ferries River ferries Dragon boats: Dragon boat racing is a popular traditional sport in Vietnam, especially during festivals and celebrations. Dragon boats are long and narrow with ornately carved dragon heads and tails. They are powered by a team of paddlers who row in unison to propel the boat forward.
